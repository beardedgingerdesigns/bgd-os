# Personal Software in the Age of AI

**URL:** https://www.aihero.dev/personal-software-is-insane-in-the-age-of-ai-u2hx2
**Published:** 2025-11-17
**Type:** Blog post

## Key Frameworks and Patterns
- **Bespoke over generic.** The future is not generic AI tools everyone uses the same way. It's bespoke applications that deeply integrate with your specific needs and projects. Personal software knows your file structure, understands your content formats, and plugs into your existing systems.
- **AI for grunt work, not thinking.** The moment you start delegating your thinking to an LLM, you're screwed. Build systems that leverage AI for the repetitive stuff. Keep the thinking for yourself.
- **Personal software as proof-of-concept.** Matt built a course video manager handling 105 videos across 41 lessons. Zero interest in making it commercial. The point: demonstrating what AI-powered personal software can do when optimized for one person's workflow.
- **Integration over intelligence.** The power isn't in the AI's capabilities alone -- it's in integrating AI into workflows you already have. Transcription, text generation, content repurposing are grunt work. Review and judgment are human work.

## Positions and Opinions
- Generic AI tools make impressive demos but fall flat in daily use because they don't know who you are or how you work.
- Don't delegate thinking to LLMs. Delegate grunt work. This is a hard line, not a suggestion.
- Personal software built for your specific workflow is the highest-value application of AI right now.
- The AI helps move faster. It doesn't replace judgment. It takes work off your hands without taking over your thinking.
- There's no need to commercialize everything. Some of the most powerful software is personal and never shipped.

## Relevant Quotes
