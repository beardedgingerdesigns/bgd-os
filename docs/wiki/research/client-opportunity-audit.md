# Client Opportunity Audit

**Curated:** 2026-06-25 | **Source research:** 2026-05-28
**Consolidates:** overnight-client-problem-audit.md

27 specific, build-ready AI automation opportunities grounded in the real operations of six existing BGD clients. This is the warm-lead version of the [market landscape](market-landscape.md): instead of chasing new mid-market buyers, monetize the relationships and systems Justin already maintains.

**How to use:** pick the 2-3 highest-leverage opportunities per client, build a one-pager, bring it to the next touchpoint. Every pitch below is phrased as Justin would say it to that client. Pricing is incremental MRR on top of existing retainers.

### Recommended sequence

1. **Terraplex (now)** — dealer marketing material is confirmed by two dealers (Pyro + New Heights, unprompted); Dakota Fest forces action. Natural BrandOS extension. Pitch in the next Terraplex touchpoint.
2. **IowaEverywhere (now)** — V2 planning is live. Content repurposing + transcript SEO are the highest-leverage features to fold into V2 scope; they grow the network's ad revenue, which makes the $250/mo retainer look cheap.
3. **Wild Rose (June)** — don't distract from the active redesign. After launch, pitch promotion automation as "Phase 3." Thermal Kitchen's HubSpot Phase 2 is the natural entry for the lead-qualification pitch.
4. **Hatch DSM (June)** — pre-fair planning starts in June. Pitch Cooper on ISF content automation + camping support bot; can expand into year-round agency workflow help.
5. **Co-Line (July)** — use the hiring pipeline as the hook to reactivate the stalled redesign: *"Forget the redesign for now. Let me solve your hiring problem and we'll talk about the site later."*
6. **NPS Media (opportunistic)** — thin relationship via Partners For Sight. Build trust by delivering on existing work; watch for Natalie mentioning reporting pain or churn.
