# Iowa Everywhere — Reference Overview

*A reference doc compiling what's known about the network, its content, team, and structure. Last reviewed against iowaeverywhere.com in May 2026.*

---

## What it is

Iowa Everywhere (iowaeverywhere.com) is a locally owned, on-demand podcast and media network based in Iowa, focused primarily on Iowa sports — Hawkeyes, Cyclones, and Big 12 coverage — with adjacent lifestyle, business, and food/beverage content. The brand tagline is "The largest personalities in Iowa sports."

The site was created roughly two years ago. It operates as a content hub with shows, editorial columns, video, and a separate merch storefront.

---

## Content / shows

### Sports (core programming)

- **Murph & Andy** — recently joined the network and is positioned as the flagship. Sports and pop-culture mix. Roughly 245 episodes at last check.
- **Two Guys** — Iowa and Iowa State sports analysis. ~208 episodes.
- **Legends & Listeners** — sports talk with heavy fan interaction. ~141 episodes.
- **The CW Pod** — broader conversational show; recent episodes have leaned into culture and tech topics. ~143 episodes.
- **Bigger Than 12** — Big 12 conference coverage. ~86 episodes.
- **Cyclone Sunday** — Iowa State–focused. ~49 episodes.
- **Instant IOWA** — Iowa coverage. ~30 episodes.
- **All Access** — Iowa coverage. ~36 episodes.
- **One on One with Keith Murphy** — long-form interviews; newer show, just getting started.

### Lifestyle / off-sports

- **Overthinking** with Mackenzie Scott — quirky cultural topics. ~72 episodes.
- **Happy Hour with Keith & Jenny** — lifestyle and products. ~44 episodes.
- **Better with Beer** — beer and food pairings. ~5 episodes.
- **Iowa Nice Guy** — ~10 episodes.
- **Buckets!** — ~17 episodes.

### BPC Streaming Network (sub-brand)

A separately branded tier of business-focused shows produced in conjunction with Business Record. Distinct from core sports content.

- Business Record Iowa News Headlines
- Made in Iowa (manufacturing)
- dsm Wealth
- Fearless (women in work and life)
- Iowa Economy
- Iowa Stops Hunger

### Editorial

- **Randy Peterson** — featured columnist. Long-time Iowa State beat writer; publishes columns on the site (recent example: speculation about Iowa State's bowl game matchup).

## Context worth carrying forward

- Murph & Andy's recent arrival is the most significant addition to the lineup and is being treated as the network's flagship anchor going forward.
- The merch store living on a separate domain creates a discoverability and UX gap between content and commerce.
- BPC Streaming Network is sub-branded business content, distinct from core sports programming — important when discussing site architecture or navigation.
- The site is approaching its two-year anniversary, and a planning/brainstorming session is being scheduled for the week of May 11 to evaluate the platform and discuss future enhancements.
