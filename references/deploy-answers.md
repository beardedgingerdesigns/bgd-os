## Relationship to BrandOS — the open structural question

**Two distinct houses, same three people:**

- **Deploy Answers** owns Backup Bison + Clappy.
- **BrandOS** is **BGD-owned** (Justin's). $1,700/mo MRR; centerpiece of the productization thesis. Not a Deploy Answers asset.

**Current BrandOS relationship (2026-06-01):** Alex + Nel are informal **thought partners only** — helping Justin flesh out BrandOS, demo the product, and gather constructive feedback. No economics, defined role, or equity.

**Emerging thesis:** as BrandOS scales, Justin will need real support; that's the likely point where Alex/Nel fold in — Alex on commercial/sales (coordinate with Jon Liebl's channel, don't collide), Nel on delivery.

**The fork to resolve before "formal operating" is fleshed out:** when Alex/Nel move from thought partner to a defined role, are they **owners** of BrandOS upside or **paid help**? And does BrandOS stay BGD-owned with them contracted/partnered in, or fold **under Deploy Answers**? Cap-table + IP diverge sharply between the two.
