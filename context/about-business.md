# About the business

## Bearded Ginger Designs (BGD)

BGD pivoted from "I build Craft websites" to **"I solve business problems for mid-market companies"** (decision 2026-05-28). AI is the accelerant — one talented operator now builds what used to take a team. If the solution is a website, fine. If it's an automation, a platform, or a tool, fine. The offering is the problem-solving, not the medium.

## Revenue streams

1. **Legacy Craft CMS clients** — ~18-month contracts, monthly recurring fees, hosting + ongoing support. Real software inside Craft: custom modules, migrations, data importers, geocoding, Freeform integrations, multi-tenant systems. Still active, not the growth engine. Contracts run out naturally.
2. **BrandOS** (proof point #1) — productized channel-marketing platform for multi-tier distribution networks. One distributor hub (Terraplex) plus dealer sites. Includes a website-builder module (the Base 44 replacement target across the portfolio). ICP: B2B manufacturers with dealer networks. Current MRR: see `context/priorities.md`.
3. **AI-powered business solutions** (proof point #2, in validation) — operational automations, workflow tools, and AI-assisted systems for mid-market companies. Jon Liebl is sales channel + first client.
