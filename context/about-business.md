# About the business

## Bearded Ginger Designs (BGD)

Custom Craft CMS builds with hosting and ongoing support. ~18-month contracts with monthly recurring fees, plus a 36-month redesign cycle as a retention mechanism. Real software inside Craft — custom modules, migrations, data importers, geocoding, Freeform integrations, multi-tenant systems.

The strategic move: building a hub-and-spoke channel marketing platform (Terraplex + dealer network) as the first productized recurring-revenue platform play. Vertical SaaS, not bespoke client work.

## ICP

Mixed today, narrowing tomorrow:

- **B2B manufacturers with dealer networks** — Terraplex and dealers (Pyro Ag, Black Knight, New Heights, Great River, Truss Services onboarding). Strategic focus. This is where the platform play lives.
- **Established publications** — ToneQuest.
- **Local/regional businesses** — Kuberski Brothers, Matting by Design, InsideOut Iowa. Legacy / non-strategic.

## Revenue

Invoicing, payments, and revenue tracking all flow through Bonsai (https://www.hellobonsai.com/). 2RM is W-2 (separate book). BGD revenue today is mostly project-based with some recurring; the productization push (Q2 priority #1) is what flips the mix toward MRR.
