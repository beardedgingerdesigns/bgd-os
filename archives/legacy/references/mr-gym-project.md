# Mr Gym — Online Store

Compiled 2026-05-23 from a grill-me kickoff-prep session with Justin. No prior Gmail/comms history surfaced; project is **pre-build**, decisions captured here will seed the project wiki and inform the next client conversation.

## What the business is

**Mr Gym** is an apparel brand owned and operated by **Bradley Banks**, a personal trainer working out of a gym in **Waukee, Iowa**. Brand name ≠ owner's name — "Mr Gym" is the brand identity, Bradley is the person.

Product: gym apparel, currently **T-shirts only**. Multiple designs, currently held inventory + sold at pop-up events at gyms around the Des Moines metro. He has done **a few successful pop-ups** but no other distribution.

## Strategic posture (locked this session)

**Now: Side hustle / supplemental income (A).**
**Aspirational: Lifestyle brand play (B), 12+ months out.**

Bradley wants the brand to eventually grow into a real lifestyle identity, but he has **no social media presence and does no business outreach**. Until he develops the personal infrastructure to drive a brand, we are explicitly building for A — lean, low-burn, low-ceremony — while keeping the foundation good enough to grow into B later without a rebuild.

**Critical constraint:** the online store has **no acquisition channel of its own**. Bradley does not market. The store's job is to be a credible backstop for pop-up customers ("can I buy this online?") and to passively capture customer info for an eventual B-mode activation.

- **Timing collision** — Q2 productization push, Wild Rose launch slip, ToneQuest launch, Thermal Kitchen launch all in the same window. Justin's calendar is tight. Don't promise a launch date before checking against these.
