# Thermal Kitchen — Project Plan & Timeline

**Drafted:** 2026-05-13
**Owner:** Justin Lobaito (BGD)
**Client lead:** Deann Gibson, Director of Sales
**Brokered by:** Aaron Harn (Wild Rose Corporate)
**Investor sign-off:** Brian Diver (Kirke LLC)
**Launch window:** Week of 2026-06-01
**Standing call:** Bi-weekly, Wed 9am CT (next: 2026-05-20)

## Objective

Relaunch the Thermal Kitchen website to reflect their repositioning from "emerging brands" toward mid-to-large size human nutrition and pet nutrition manufacturers. Site needs to read at that altitude. Phase 2 (June) wires the site into HubSpot for lead qualification and post-submission content flow.

## Phase split

- **Phase 1 (May):** Site design update, proper service reflection, leverage existing blog content. Tight scope, launch end-of-May / week-of-June 1.
- **Phase 2 (June):** HubSpot + website funnel integration. Lead qualification, post-submission communication, content-to-customer pipeline.
