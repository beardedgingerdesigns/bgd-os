## Edit decisions for Justin to react to

1. **Subject line.** Pick one of the four above, or write your own.
2. **First-name personalization.** HubSpot supports `[First Name]` tokens. Confirm the field exists in your dealer records.
3. **"Coming soon" line about the dealer portal.** Are you comfortable making this promise? It's a real demand signal from Pyro + NH, but it's also a brainstorm item, not a shipping feature. Two options:
   - Keep as written (creates buyer interest, but creates expectation).
   - Soften to "I'm collecting dealer input on what marketing and trade-show support would actually help. If that's interesting to you, mention it in your reply."
   - Cut entirely. Lean on the current value prop.
4. **CTA mechanic.** Need to decide:
   - Calendar link (Calendly / similar) — I don't see one set up yet, would need to create

5. **Sender posture.** Email from `justin@beardedgingerdesigns.com` or a HubSpot-routed address? Plain text or branded HTML? My recommendation: plain text, from your real address. Higher reply rates for B2B in your audience.
